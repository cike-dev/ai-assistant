Course Guides
Your Course Guide contains all the essential information you need to understand how your chosen course operates.

Course Guides can be accessed on e:Vision by navigating to the 'My Study - Course' page and clicking on the 'Course Info' link below the Course Details.
Note that these course guides relate to current course content and may change in the future.

For more specific course information or if planning to apply for future years, contact the Faculty or Institute that runs your course.

Pls visit https://www.wlv.ac.uk/current-students/course-guides/ for more information.
