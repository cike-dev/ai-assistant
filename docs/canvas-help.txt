Accessing Canvas:
Canvas can be accessed through the Student Portal (myWLV, http://my.wlv.ac.uk/) for on-campus undergraduate, postgraduate, and postgraduate research students. Other means include Canvas desktop icon on University Windows computers OR 'Canvas' app on Apple (iOS) and Android mobile devices.

How to Log in to Canvas:
Log in using University IT credentials (email address or user ID and password) with Multifactor Authentication (MFA) set up. More information at https://www.wlv.ac.uk/its/essentials/mfa/
Complete enrolment and module registration in e:Vision to access Canvas; may take up to 24 hours after enrolment.

For support for Enrolment and Module Registration Issues, Contact ASK@WLV for issues with enrolment or module registration. Drop in at:
